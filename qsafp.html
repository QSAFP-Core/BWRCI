The Validator Network

QSAFP Validators Network
Empowering Humanity Over AI

Every AI node is a contract. Every reauth, a renewal of human trust. The QVN distributes the power of AI oversight to real people — across the globe.

How Validation Works

1

Biometric General Logon

Validator authenticates with hardware-secured biometric identity before entering the network.

2

Random Validator Pool Assignment

Renewal review assigned to random validator pool. First 3 responders are selected to proceed.

3

Biometric Confirmation of Winners

Selected validators re-authenticate to access secured logs for independent review.

4

✅ Review Logs & Vote

Validators independently review secured AI activity logs and vote to Reauth ✅ or Deny ✅.

5

Reward & Payment

System records the vote, rewards the validator, confirms payment within 24 hours.

6

Public Resolution

AI NODE REAUTHED or AI NODE DENIED — resolution is always public.
